Perseverance and Growth

Even today, undocumented students across the country continue to struggle with resources limiting their ability to attend college and advance their education. They not only struggle to deal with a sense of identity but also struggle with coming up with the finances to attend school. These students are often hardworking and diligent and should not lose the right to advance their education just because of a label. As of 2021, the number of undocumented students in the United States remains stable at ~400,000.
